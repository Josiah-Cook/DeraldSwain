Criminal Justice Class!

A fun class that teaches students about the laws, military, and government. It has 3 different levels known as Criminal Justice I, Criminal Justice II, and Criminal Justice III.


Criminal Justice Club!

A fun club that grants students more oppurtunites to experience events about law and military. It hosts various events for the club members and anyone at the highschool can join, with no need to be in the class.


About Derald Swain!

The teacher and sponsor of Criminal Justice. He is a army veteran and now serves in the Nation Guard.
